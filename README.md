# IsometKit for Blender

Welcome to **IsometKit**, a Blender add-on for easily distributing and managing isometric assets! Designed to streamline your 3D workflow, IsometKit lets you effortlessly create beautiful isometric rooms and quickly populate them with objects right from the Blender viewport.

## Features

- **Asset Loader & Browser:** Seamlessly drag-and-drop or click to place assets from various categories (Furniture, Tech, Plants, Props).
- **Smart Placement:** Assets automatically align their rotation to the surface normal they are placed on, saving you the hassle of manual tweaking.
- **Isometric Camera Setup:** Generate a perfect orthographic/isometric camera angle with a single click.
- **Quick Lighting:** Add a simple, effective lighting setup instantly to illuminate your isometric scenes.
- **Search & Filter:** Find exactly what you need with the built-in search bar and category filters.

## Installation

1. Download the `IsometKit.zip` file.
2. In Blender, go to **Edit** > **Preferences** > **Add-ons**.
3. Click **Install...** at the top right, and select the downloaded `IsometKit.zip` file.
4. Check the box next to **3D View: IsometKit** to enable it.
5. Save your Preferences!

## Quick Start

1. Open the **N-Panel** in the 3D Viewport (press `N`) and click on the **IsometKit** tab.
2. Under **Room Setup**, select a room base and click **Generate Iso Setup** to get your camera ready. Add lighting with **Generate Simple Light Setup**.
3. Under **Asset Loader**, choose a Category or use the Search bar to find an asset.
4. Select the asset from the grid and click **Place Asset**.
5. Move your mouse to position the asset. Use `Scroll Wheel` to scale, `Ctrl + Scroll` to rotate, and `Left Click` to confirm placement!
